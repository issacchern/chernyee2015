The Story of Us

It all started a year ago. She was a mutual friend of mine and we met during a day trip with my fellow friends. I didn't quite know about her at first, but after chatting for some time, I realized that she was the right person for me and then I started wooing her. A month after her birthday, I asked her out for a date and asked her to be my girlfriend. That's how we got together as couple and began our love journey ...

First Met at Darien Lake

We met during a trip to Darien Lake with my friends. I was attracted to her in every way, her personality and her beautiful smile... I just couldn't take my eyes off of her. I felt that she would be the right one for me. It was really a wonderful time meeting her and hanging out with her.

First Date at Fall Fest Carnival

It was a lovely and wonderful night at UB Fall Fest Carnival. We got a lot of small gifts from playing games, we also enjoyed the bonfire and firework. Before we left the school, I asked her to be my girlfriend and she said yes! Afterwards, we snuggled together and kissed like crazy in car. It was indeed a fun and memorable day for both of us.

A Trip to Letchworth State Park

We followed along with ISSS group to Letchworth State Park to enjoy the magnificent fall season. We brought some sandwiches and fruits for the picnic. It was a tiring trip but totally worth it.

Spring Break Getaway!

A short getaway to Rochester for Chinese awesome Dim Sum and we also went to Science Musuem during spring break.

❤ My BiiBii's Graduation! ❤

It was my girlfriend's graduation day! We took some pictures together after her graduation ceremony. I was very proud to see her wearing the graduation gown and she definitely looked amazing and beautiful!

Trip to Ohio State!

During summer break, we went to Ohio State for a short break from studies. We went to Cedar Point Amusement Park as well as the National Zoo at Ohio.

Reunion at NYC!

It has been a month long since I was with my girlfriend at Buffalo. This time, I came all the way to her hometown at Binghamton to visit her and meet her parents. The next morning, we began our trip to NYC for room hunting and vacation as well. Even though we aren't together physically, I don't feel like it would affect our relationship. In fact, it would make our love stronger than ever!

Short Thanksgiving Reunion

I travelled to NYC to meet up with my girlfriend during Thanksgiving break. We spent most of our time being together, hanging out at nearby places and on top of all, hunting down some good food! Although it was a short stay at NYC, it felt so good to be physically in touch with my girlfriend and I promised we will be seeing each other again very soon.

Revisiting Binghamton

It was my second time visiting my girlfriend's parents in Binghamton. I brought some wines for them and we had a really nice and warm Christmas Eve's dinner together that night. Yeah, we got a selfie stick!

Visiting Cornell University

We went to visit Cornell University at Ithaca, NY which was about an hour drive from Binghamton. We took tons of pictures of mother natures, old historic buildings, macro shots and selfies . Well I have to say, we really well-prepared for it: powerbank for charging phone, special lens for different shots and selfie stick for selfie time!

Back to NYC!

After a short vacation back in Binghamton, we now headed back to NYC. I am so glad that everything had worked out so well for us. We have been together for days and it is still counting! It's such a bless to meet you at the right place at the right time. I would be nothing if it wasn't for you. I love you babe ❤
